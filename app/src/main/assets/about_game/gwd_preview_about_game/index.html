Once upon a time, an old country lived an old king who had a beautiful princess.

Suddenly one day, a lot of scary and evil monsters came to the country, took the princess away and imprisoned in a tower, the Tower of Evils. 

14The king sent a bundle of warriors to rescue the princess, but they never went back. Finally, the king raised the warriors, saying if he brought the princess back, he would be marrying the princess. You have decided to go to the tower of evils to save the princess.
